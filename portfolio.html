Shinnosuke Nohara is 5 years old and goes to kindergarten. He goes wild every day with his unique friends in the Kasukabe Defense Organization and his little sister, Himawari. His mom, dad, and teachers often find themselves caught up in the chaos that Shinchan effortlessly creates!
