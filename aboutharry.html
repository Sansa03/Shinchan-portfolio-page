Hiroshi Nohara

About Me

Hiroshi Nohara is the father of Shinnosuke Nohara (Shinchan). He works at the Kasukabe Construction Company as a salaryman.

Age

40 years old

Family

Hiroshi is married to Misae Nohara and has two children, Shinchan and Himawari.

Job

Hiroshi works as a salaryman at the Kasukabe Construction Company. He is often portrayed as a hardworking but somewhat clumsy and forgetful individual.
